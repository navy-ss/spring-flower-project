About Our School

Spring Flowers School is one of the most prestigious and esteemed schools in the city.

We are a leading artistic educational provider, offering in-depth learning opportunities for both students and teachers looking to develop their skills. Currently, there are over 50 teachers, 3 Counselors and 3 Administrators who provide a quality education to approximately 850 students. Spring flower school colors are purple & gold and we are known as the Titans. Some of the co-curricular activities offered by the faculty and staff are: Concert Band, Ukulele Band, VEX Robotics, Art Club, Builders Club and Track and Field. Students also have the opportunity to participate in Science Fair, Math Counts, Newbery Quiz Bowl, Geography Bee, HIKI NŌ Video Productions and Student Body or Class Government. Spring Flowers Students consistently demonstrate excellence as evidenced by their outstanding achievements in many of the aforementioned activities. Spring Flowers School utilizes a teaming structure to best support student growth and achievement. There are two teams per grade level that consist of a language arts teacher, math teacher, social studies teacher, science teacher, and elective teacher.
